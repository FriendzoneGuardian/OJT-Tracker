# Welcome to the OJT-Tracker Wiki! 🚀

The **OJT-Tracker** (by FriendzoneGuardian) is a premium, high-performance tool designed for students and interns to track their Required Experiential Learning (OJT) hours with mathematical precision.

## 🌟 Key Features
- **Smart Trajectory**: Not just a calculator, but a calendar-aware simulator that predicts your exact completion date.
- **Weekend Flexibility**: Working on Saturdays? Toggle them in settings.
- **Holiday Awareness**: Pre-loaded with 2026 Philippine holidays, fully customizable.
- **"On Wheels" Mobility**: Portable data and standalone builds for IT-friendly deployment.
