# 📖 User Guide

## ⏱️ Logging Hours
1. Click the **Log Entry** button (Top Right).
2. Use the **Preset Buttons** (Full Day, Half Day) for speed, or manually enter times.
3. The table updates instantly. Hover over entries to see delete/edit options.

## 🗓️ Smart Trajectory (Expected End)
The "Expected End" date in your sidebar is calculated using a **Simulated Calendar Walk**:
- It looks at your **Last 14 days** average.
- It steps through the future calendar day-by-day.
- It **skips** non-working days (Weekends, Holidays, and your custom Exclusions).
- It stops exactly when your `Target Hours` are reached.

## ⚙️ Settings & Weekends
Need to include Saturdays in your work week?
1. Click the **Gear Icon** in the sidebar.
2. Toggle **Include Sats** or **Include Suns**.
3. Hit **Save**. Your trajectory will recalculate immediately.

## 📅 Managing Holidays
Use the **Calendar Icon** to manage non-working dates:
- **Holidays**: Shared national dates (Auto-cleans when past).
- **Exclusions**: Personal skip-dates (Sick days, personal leaves).
- **Pagination**: Use the search and page controls to manage large lists.
